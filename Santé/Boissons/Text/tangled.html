Traduction française

J'aime eating des viennoiseries for breakfast car they are délicieuses and sweet. Mon frère prefers un biscuit with his coffee, mais I find that le chocolat chaud est more comforting, surtout in winter. En été, nothing is better qu'une glace rafraîchissante. For celebrations, ma mère always makes un grand gâteau, and we enjoy des petit-fours after the meal. Enfin, a glass de jus d'orange frais is perfect pour commencer the day.
